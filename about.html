About

My name is Enrico Cesarin, and I enjoy creating music and digital art. I first became interested in this field by producing electronic music, exploring genres such as trap, ambient, and experimental music. Later, I decided to deepen my interests by studying Music and New Technologies at the Conservatory of Trieste, where I earned my bachelor’s degree in 2023 and my master’s degree in 2026. Throughout this academic journey, I discovered a strong passion for multimodality, focusing on the study and development of interactive systems for artistic research and creative practice. ards ambitious creative goals, collaborating with like-minded people, and exploring new artistic direction.
