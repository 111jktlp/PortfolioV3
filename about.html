About

I'm Enrico Cesarin, a multidisciplinary artist working across music, visual design and digital media.

My work explores the intersection between sound, image and emotion, blending contemporary aesthetics with experimental approaches.

Over the years, I've been involved in projects ranging from music and audiovisual productions to interactive experiences and artistic installations.

I'm always interested in working towards ambitious creative goals, collaborating with like-minded people, and exploring new artistic directions. Meeting new artists, sharing ideas, and creating things together is one of the aspects of the creative process I value most.
