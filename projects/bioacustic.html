Overview

Bio_Acoustic_Ritual24 is an immersive electroacoustic composition designed for a 24-channel loudspeaker system. Conceived as a sonic exploration of an imaginary natural environment, the work blurs the boundaries between the tangible and the abstract, inviting the listener into a continuously evolving acoustic landscape. Through an intricate interplay of environmental recordings and synthesized sounds, the piece creates a hybrid ecosystem where familiar natural elements coexist with artificial and mystical sonic presences.

The composition unfolds as a spatial journey, transforming the listening space into a living environment populated by moving sound objects, shifting textures, and emerging sonic rituals. Suspended between reality and imagination, Bio_Acoustic_Ritual24 explores the perceptual relationship between nature, technology, and spatial listening, immersing the audience in a constantly changing auditory experience.

Process

The work was conceived specifically for a multichannel diffusion system, with spatialization serving as a fundamental compositional parameter rather than a mere technical enhancement. Various audio layers were carefully composed and positioned within predetermined locations of the sound field, creating stable points of reference throughout the piece. Alongside these fixed elements, other sound materials move continuously through space, following automatically generated trajectories with variable speeds and directions, producing a dynamic and immersive sense of motion. The sonic material combines field recordings captured in rural environments—including birds, toads, and other natural sound sources—with electronically synthesized textures. These contrasting elements interact to create a dialogue between the real and the imagined, between acoustic familiarity and artificial transformation. The synthesized sounds emerge from and dissolve into the environmental recordings, generating fluid transitions and evocative atmospheres that reinforce the ritualistic and contemplative character of the work. By integrating environmental documentation with spatial composition and electronic sound design, Bio_Acoustic_Ritual24 transforms the listening environment into an immersive sonic habitat, where natural and synthetic worlds intertwine within a continuously evolving three-dimensional soundscape.
