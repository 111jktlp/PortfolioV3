Overview

Love Failure Ambivalence is a collaborative audiovisual work by Enrico Cesarin and Linda Mazzolini, developed through the intersection of visual storytelling and sound design. Created with Enrico Cesarin as director and sound designer and Linda Mazzolini as art director and performer, the project explores the expressive possibilities that emerge when image, movement, and sound are conceived as parts of a unified artistic language. The work centers on the symbolic journey of a pair of shoes, which becomes the visual representation of an inner emotional process. Through a sequence of evocative situations and transformations, the object evolves into a metaphor for personal attachment, change, and rediscovery.

The journey portrayed in the video reflects the moment when a forgotten passion unexpectedly returns. It represents the spark that reignites after something once deeply loved gradually loses its appeal, or when something long desired and finally obtained eventually falls out of favor and ceases to hold meaning. Years later, however, it is rediscovered and reimagined, taking on a new form and significance. Through this symbolic path, the work explores themes of attachment, loss, transformation, and renewed appreciation.

Process

The project was developed through the close integration of sound and image, with both elements contributing equally to the construction of meaning. The visual narrative follows the symbolic movement of the shoes through different situations and environments, while the soundtrack reinforces and expands the emotional dimension of the journey. Particular attention was given to the sonic aspect of the work, which became the primary vehicle for expressing the evolving emotional states represented in the video. Sound design, editing, and the relationship between audio events and visual actions were carefully shaped to create a cohesive audiovisual experience, allowing the audience to perceive the transformation of the symbolic object beyond its purely visual representation. By combining a simple visual subject with a more expressive and detailed sonic treatment, Love Failure Ambivalence investigates how personal interests, desires, and passions can fade over time and later return in unexpected ways. The work ultimately reflects on the cyclical nature of emotional attachment and the possibility of rediscovering meaning through transformation and distance.
